Congratulation! You have successfully 
signed up for spndr!

To use spndr, all you have to do is 
follow what we tell you. To start a 
new transaction, simply send 'new' 
and we will start a transaction for 
you!

If you want to abort an ongoing
transaction, send '!abort' and we 
will stop the transaction.

You can look at your recent purchases
with 'show'.

If you ever get stuck, send '!help'
and we will be there to guide you.

happy spndng!
